v2.3.1: Fix uninstallable/corrupt Android APK by signing the release build. The CI now generates a keystore and Gradle signs the release APK (v1+v2+v3), and a verify step runs apksigner. Previously assembleRelease produced an unsigned app-release-unsigned.apk that Android rejects as corrupt. Also bumps versions to 2.3.1 (versionCode 5).
